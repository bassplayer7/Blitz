Tips

  • Tap score to change player's color. Now you can match your color with your card color.
  • Use the up/down arrows above your keyboard to move from score to score. It will assume it is a positive score.
  • Your game is always saved to your device. Go ahead and Google who invented Dutch Blitz® and then pick up here where you left off.
  • There's no need to tap complete round every time. It takes care of it automatically.
